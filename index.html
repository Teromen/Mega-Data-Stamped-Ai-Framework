Game Engine: Tag & Trace Core

This custom game engine focuses on lightweight, deterministic rendering with intelligent systems for AI, lighting, and physics — all centered on a powerful tagging and tracing architecture.

Core Feature Set

  • Surface Tagging: Prebaked metadata tags per texture or mesh surface — controls AI vision, lighting detail, and physics interaction.
  • Texture-Based Lighting LOD: Lightmaps augmented by tags to enable dynamic lighting precision at runtime.
  • Trace-Triggered AI: Bot sight, hearing, and pathing react to tagged visibility zones and shadow layers.
  • Deterministic Ray Sampling: AI line-of-sight and sound tracing calculated from fixed, replicable mesh tags.
  • Stamped Physics Zones: Low-cost surface stamps embed physical material and friction data.
  • Simple Reverb & Sound Tagging: Sound environments derived from material tags and occlusion layers.

Design Philosophy

The engine prioritizes realism over complexity. All interactive behavior and lighting is driven by tagged data — no hidden magic, no unnecessary systems. This keeps things simple and transparent, while still delivering high-fidelity, trace-aware simulation.

Development Goals

  • Compete with projected next-gen standards (5-year forecast)
  • Benchmark all systems with bandwidth throughput in mind
  • Enable AI training through light-aware, tagged simulation
  • Prebake all possible texture metadata before final build
