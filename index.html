Tag + Trace Engine Core

Simple, realistic, and modular game engine focused on tag-based data optimization and surface-aware AI.

Key Engine Systems

  • Surface Tagging System – Embed metadata into textures and meshes for AI vision, physics logic, and light behavior.
  • Trace-Based AI Perception – Bot logic uses raytraced data and surface tags for vision, sound detection, and navigation.
  • Texture-Driven Lighting Control – Normalmaps, masks, and texture tags influence real-time lighting fidelity.
  • LOD-Based Light Tagging – Real-time raytracing adjusts detail dynamically based on surface tags.
  • Baked Lightmap Overrides – RT systems can selectively overwrite or blend with baked lighting based on tags.
  • Prebaked Physics Zones – Stamp areas with physical properties before runtime to accelerate simulation.
  • Tagged Sound Reverb Zones – Acoustic environments controlled through tagged geometry.

Design Philosophy

This engine isn't about complexity — it's about purpose. Every texture, every surface, every effect: informed by tags. Built for realistic bots, efficient rendering, and total runtime clarity.
